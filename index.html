And Here's a little about moi

I am a front-end developer from Montreal, QC, and I graduated from chemical engineer. I am a curious individual who is passionate about science, math and technology... Yep! I'm a nerd.

The one thing I loved about engineering was designing and developing processes. I chose to achieve this challenge by applying these key properties: creativity, determination, innovation, passion and most importantly, sweat & tears.

In 2015, I finally completed my M.A.Sc. and entered the job market. Fast forward to October 2016, when I went rogue and I started front-end development full-time and full-blast!

The main reason why I love making websites is that it combines technical skills and creative ones. It would be my pleasure to build something awesome for you in your image, as your front-end developer.

I live on coffee and music. They are the two things that fuel my work.
